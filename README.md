Blinkit Data Analysis using Excel

Key Performance Metrics:
1. Total Sales: $1.20 Million
2. Average Sales per Transaction: $141
3. Total Number of Items Sold: 8,523
4. Average Customer Rating: 4.0

Key Insights from the Dashboard:

Sales Trend by Outlet Establishment Year:
1. Highest sales recorded in 2018 ($204.5K).
2. Stable growth observed from 2012–2017.
3. Slight decline after 2018 but maintained consistent performance.

Sales by Outlet Size:
1. Medium-sized outlets generated the highest sales contribution.
2. Followed by Small outlets, while High-size outlets contributed comparatively less.

Sales by Outlet Location:
1. Tier 3 locations generated the highest revenue.
2. Tier 2 performed moderately.
3. Tier 1 locations had the lowest contribution among the three.

Sales by Fat Content:
1. Regular items contributed 65% ($776.3K) of total sales.
2. Low-fat items contributed 35% ($425.2K).
3. Customers prefer regular fat products more.

Sales by Item Type:

**Top performing categories:
1. Fruits & Vegetables – $178.1K
2. Snack Foods – $175.4K
3. Household – $136.0K
4. Frozen Foods – $118.6K

**Lower performing categories:
1. Seafood
2. Breakfast
3. Starchy Foods

Outlet Type Performance:
1. Supermarket Type 1 generated the highest number of items sold (5,577).
2. Supermarket Type 3 generated the highest total sales.
